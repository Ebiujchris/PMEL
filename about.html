About Us

PMEL Advisory Centre is a regional consultancy and advisory firm providing high-quality services in Programme Management, Monitoring & Evaluation, Research, Learning, Organizational Development, and Capacity Strengthening.

We work with governments, INGOs, UN agencies, development partners and the private sector to design, implement, monitor and evaluate high-impact programmes that transform lives.

Our Mission

To empower organizations and communities with evidence, innovative solutions and practical support for lasting impact and sustainable development.

Our Vision

A world where evidence drives decisions and communities thrive in resilience, equity and opportunity.
