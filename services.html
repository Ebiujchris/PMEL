Our Services

Comprehensive consultancy across key development sectors

PMEL Advisory Centre provides high-quality, evidence-driven consultancy, technical management support, research, and advisory services that strengthen the effectiveness, accountability, and impact of development and humanitarian interventions across Africa.

Public Health & Health Systems Strengthening

We support health systems strengthening initiatives through evidence-based interventions, policy development, and capacity building. Our work includes health programme design, implementation support, monitoring and evaluation of health interventions, and strengthening health information systems.

  • Health programme design and management
  • Health systems assessments and strengthening
  • Disease surveillance and response systems
  • Community health programming

Climate Change Adaptation & Resilience

Climate adaptation programming and resilience building for sustainable communities. We support climate-smart interventions, disaster risk reduction, and community resilience programming across vulnerable populations.

  • Climate vulnerability assessments
  • Climate adaptation strategy development
  • Disaster risk reduction programming
  • Community resilience building

Agriculture, Food Security & Livelihoods

Livelihoods enhancement and food security interventions for rural communities. We provide technical support in agricultural value chain development, market systems strengthening, and sustainable livelihoods programming.

  • Agricultural value chain analysis
  • Food security assessments
  • Livelihoods programming and market systems
  • Rural development initiatives

Gender Equality & Social Inclusion

Promoting inclusive development and social inclusion across all interventions. We support gender mainstreaming, women's empowerment programming, and social inclusion strategies.

  • Gender analysis and mainstreaming
  • Women's empowerment programming
  • Social inclusion strategies
  • Youth development initiatives

Humanitarian Response & Refugee Protection

Emergency response, refugee protection, and resilience building in crisis contexts. We provide technical support in humanitarian needs assessments, emergency response programming, and refugee integration.

  • Humanitarian needs assessments
  • Emergency response programming
  • Refugee protection and integration
  • Humanitarian-development nexus programming

Mental Health & Psychosocial Support

MHPSS programming for vulnerable populations and crisis-affected communities. We support mental health system strengthening, psychosocial support interventions, and community-based mental health programming.

  • MHPSS needs assessments
  • Mental health programme design
  • Community-based psychosocial support
  • Mental health capacity building

Community Development & Social Protection

Supporting community-led development initiatives and social protection systems. We provide technical assistance in community mobilization, social protection programming, and local governance strengthening.

  • Community development programming
  • Social protection systems strengthening
  • Local governance and participation
  • Community-based organizations support
