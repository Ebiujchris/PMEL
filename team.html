Our Team Strength

Our resident consultants provide continuous technical leadership, ensuring consistency, responsiveness, and quality assurance, while our technical consultants bring specialized expertise, global best practices, and sector-specific knowledge to complex assignments.

The research and consultancy team strengthens our work through rigorous analysis, monitoring and evaluation, policy research, and data-driven learning, enabling the generation of credible evidence and actionable insights.

Together, the team combines interdisciplinary expertise, practical field experience, and a shared commitment to excellence, enabling PMEL Advisory Centre to deliver innovative, integrated, and context-responsive solutions for governments, development partners, UN agencies, NGOs, and private sector actors across Africa.
